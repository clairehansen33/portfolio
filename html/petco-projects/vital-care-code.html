Vet Exams

Regular vet exams can help detect illnesses and diseases early on so you avoid costly vet bills in the future. Up-to-date vaccinations will help ensure a long, happy life with your pet. Your vet will do a nose-to-tail exam to help make sure your pet looks and feels their best.

Grooming

Help care for your pet's physical health with a full-service groom that includes deep-cleaning shampoo, blow-dry, 15-minute brushout, scented spritz, gland expression and nail trim, plus a breed-specific cut and style if you choose a bath with haircut.

Customized grooming packages include Flea Cleanse, Shed Release, Calm & Refresh and more. Some additional à la carte add-ons are teeth-brushing, breath refresh and ear cleaning.

Dog Nail Trimming (Part of a Full-Service Groom)

Regularly trimming your pet’s nails can help prevent discomfort while walking and injuries caused by them not properly gripping the ground. It can also help minimize carpet damage and skin injuries. Our stylists will help make sure your pet’s nails are a safe and healthy length.

Dog Teeth-Brushing (Possible Add-On to a Full-Service Groom)

Routine teeth-brushing in dogs helps prevent plaque and tartar build-up, gingivitis and gum disease. It also helps minimize bad breath, tooth decay and infections so your pet enjoys a healthy smile. Our stylists can also help spot broken or fractured teeth before they become larger health issues.
