Vital Care Landing Page

WholeHearted is Petco's own dog food brand. A wide assortment of affordable food and treat options for both dogs and cats makes WholeHearted a great option for all of Petco's customers. We decided to build a landing page to support this and teach our users all about the brand. The page has several different modules that highlight all different aspects of the products, including an brand comparison table, a slider of ingredients, a list of flavors, transition instructions, and reviews.

I coded this page using HTML, CSS and Javscript. I replicated the design that was given to me by our designers in XD. Click the link below to view the whole page on Petco's website. This page has since been completely renovated by one of my teammates to match our newest Vital Care campaign, so unfortunately I cannot link to the live page on Petco's website, but the below link will take you to a version I have included in this website.
